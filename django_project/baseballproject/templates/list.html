  • Go to fanduel.com, draftkings.com or yahoo.com/dailyfantasy (wait until at least 9AM EST before running this optimizer, otherwise projected points may not be available!)
  • Find an MLB contest. This optimizer is for the Standard/Classic MLB contests (no 'mixup' Fanduel contests and no 'arcade' Draftkings contests)
  • Enter the contest (on Yahoo click the contest name to access the csv download option)
  • Click 'Download players list' on Fanduel, 'Export To CSV' on Draftkings, 'Export players list' on Yahoo (pictured below, respectively)

  • Come back to this website
  • Select the contest website you are using from the drop down menu
  • Click the 'Browse' button, and select the csv file
  • Click the 'Submit' button, and it will output the optimal lineup for your contest (using projected points from Fangraphs.com)
